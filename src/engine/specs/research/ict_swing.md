## Consensus Rules

1. **HTF bias via market structure**: Weekly/daily trend direction established through swing highs/lows (HH/HL = bullish, LH/LL = bearish). All 5 sources agree.
2. **Premium/discount zone**: Longs only in discount zone (below equilibrium/50%), shorts only in premium zone (above equilibrium/50%). 4 of 5 sources explicit.
3. **Liquidity sweep as catalyst**: A sweep of prior swing highs (BSL) or lows (SSL) precedes the entry setup. The sweep provides the institutional trigger. 4 of 5 sources.
4. **BOS/MSS confirmation**: After the sweep, a break of structure or market structure shift on the entry timeframe confirms the move. All sources agree.
5. **PD array entry (OB or FVG)**: Enter at an order block or FVG within the premium/discount zone after BOS. All sources agree on PD array as the entry trigger.
6. **No conventional indicators**: ICT swing trades are purely price-action/structure based. ADX, RSI, MACD, etc. are not part of the methodology. 4 of 5 sources explicit.
7. **Targets = next liquidity pool**: Exit at opposing BSL/SSL or major HTF PD array. All sources agree.
8. **Stop beyond sweep extreme**: Stop loss placed beyond the liquidity sweep candle's high/low. 3 of 5 explicit.

## Final Definition

**ICT Swing Trade** is a multi-day position entry model based on institutional order flow concepts. It differs from ICT intraday/scalp models by using higher-timeframe structure (daily/4H) for bias and targeting HTF liquidity pools.

**Setup conditions:**
1. **HTF market structure bias** — bullish (HH/HL on daily) or bearish (LH/LL on daily) trend established via swing detection and BOS.
2. **Premium/discount filter** — longs only when price is in the discount zone (below equilibrium), shorts only in the premium zone (above equilibrium).
3. **Liquidity sweep trigger** — price sweeps prior swing high (BSL) or swing low (SSL), taking out resting stop orders.
4. **BOS/MSS confirmation** — after the sweep, a break of structure on the entry timeframe (4H/1H) confirms institutional intent.
5. **PD array entry** — enter at an order block or FVG that sits within the discount (for longs) or premium (for shorts) zone.

**Execution:**
- Enter at the PD array (OB or FVG) after BOS confirmation post-sweep.
- Stop loss beyond the liquidity sweep extreme.
- Target: next opposing liquidity pool (BSL for longs, SSL for shorts) or major HTF PD array.
- Exit also on opposing BOS (structure break against position).

**What this is NOT:**
- Not an OTE-only strategy (OTE fib is optional enhancement, not core).
- Not indicator-dependent (no ADX, RSI, or MACD filters).
- Not an intraday model (holds beyond session, targets HTF liquidity).
