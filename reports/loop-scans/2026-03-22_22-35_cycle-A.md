### Confirmed Bugs — FIXED

**BUG P2: backtester.py:1528 — Recovery days off-by-one (includes trough day)**
- `range(dd_trough_idx, len(equity))` starts AT the trough, making recovery_days always 1 too high. A strategy recovering in exactly 5 days reports 6, hitting the `> 5` rejection in performance_gate.
- **Fix:** Changed to `range(dd_trough_idx + 1, len(equity))`.

**BUG P2: prop_sim.py:367-412 — Double-deducts ongoing fees from payout projection**
- `payout_projection` (line 370) already subtracts `monthly_fee`. Then `annual_gross_payout = payout_projection * 12` is computed, and `annual_ongoing_fees` is subtracted AGAIN on line 412.
- Impact: Apex ($85/mo) and FFN ($126/mo) projections are $1,020/yr and $1,512/yr too pessimistic.
- **Fix:** Removed second `annual_ongoing_fees` deduction from `true_net_annual_payout`.

**BUG P2: backtester.py:1591 + performance_gate.py:151 — TIER_3 drawdown threshold wrong**
- Both use `max_dd < 2000` for TIER_3 but CLAUDE.md spec says `< $2,500`. Strategies with dd $2,000-$2,500 incorrectly REJECTED instead of TIER_3.
- **Fix:** Changed both to `< 2500`.
